NMEASimulator

NMEASimulator is a FREE Chrome Application for Google Chrome / ChromeOS for generating NMEA data streams to devices connected via TCP/IP, WebSocket or Serial port.

NMEASimulator is simple and easy to use generating NMEA sentences simulating the following:
  • GPS Fix Quality,HDOP, PDOP & VDOP, Number of satellites, Current local time and date.
  • Vessel position, speed and heading data
  • Wind speed and direction
  • Water temperature and depth
  • Waypoint position and name
